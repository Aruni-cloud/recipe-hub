Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Whipping cream

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate and let it cool.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar.
  3. Fold in whipped cream and beaten egg whites.
  4. Chill for 2 hours before serving.

Fruit Salad with Honey-Lime Dressing

Ingredients:

  • Mixed fruits (berries, melon, grapes)
  • Honey
  • Lime juice
  • Mint leaves

Steps:

  1. Chop fruits and place in a bowl.
  2. Mix honey and lime juice for dressing.
  3. Pour dressing over fruits and toss gently.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.

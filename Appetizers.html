Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Baguette slices

Steps:

  1. Chop tomatoes, garlic, and basil.
  2. Mix with olive oil.
  3. Toast baguette slices.
  4. Top with tomato mixture and serve.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • Button mushrooms
  • Cream cheese
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Remove mushroom stems and chop them.
  2. Mix stems with cream cheese, garlic, and parsley.
